The Flag

Zen Story

Two monks were arguing about a flag that stood high in the distance.

“It’s the wind that is wavering,” stated the first one. “No, it is the flag that is wavering,” contended the second.

A Zen master, who happened to be walking by, overheard the debate and interrupted them. “Neither the flag nor the wind is wavering,” he said, “It is your minds that is wavering.”
